How to prepare lemon water

Lemon water is a natural kind of juice, just a combination of lemon and water without any artificial ingredients that helps us from getting dehydrated. As the making process is easy and helps in many ways to our body,hence I like it to have everyday.

Ingredients

  • lemon
  • water(H2O)
  • sugar/salt/honey

Procedure

  1. First squeez the lemons in a jar 🍋
  2. now, mix water and lemon juice 🥃
  3. next, add sugar/salt/honey as per your requirement 🍯
  4. now, stir the mixture with spoon 🥄
  5. Thats it, lemon water is ready.. cheers 🥂.

This is very simple to do and healthy. Looks similar to water with lemon pulp and taste depends on sugar/salt/honey. A little mixed taste of sour with sweetness/salty. Hope you try it to have everyday and keep yourself healthy
